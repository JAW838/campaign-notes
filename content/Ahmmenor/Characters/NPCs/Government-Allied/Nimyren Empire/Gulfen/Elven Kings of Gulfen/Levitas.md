- 4th King of the Elves
- Reportedly descended from the old world.
- Was King of the region when the warring factions were united into the Empire by [[Emperor Ballas]].
- Aided in his rule by a [[Gulfen#Councilors|Council of 5 Elvish nobility]] 
# Royal Liaison
The right hand man of the King, [[Dezeran Thain]] is a trusted advisor of the King and rules the high council in his stead when he is unavailable.
# Councilors
## Lyshann Mithrandir
A Pallid Elf Tasked with overseeing the Illuminate Academy. The Symbol of her station is the crescent moon upon a starry sky.
## Aelon Zephyr
A wood elf tasked with maintaining the old forest, the symbol of his station is an Elder Tree.
## Kybern Dassior
The Diplomat. A sun Elf only recently named to the Council, the symbol of his station is that of the Sun. Is known to have a half elven step daughter named Esta. He recently encountered the Party during the Summer Solstice.
## Lenor Bailium
An Eladrin tasked with maintaining elvish culture & their connection to the feywild. The symbol of their station is the Planar Rune of the Feywild.
## Unknown
A very private figure, tasked with overseeing [[Gulfen]]’s Military contribution to the [[The Nimyren Empire|Empire]].
