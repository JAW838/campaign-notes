Queen of the Underdark, home of the Shadar-Kai.
Worshipped by [[Corvel]] and [[Reen Voidswift]].
Connected to blood magic.
# Timeline
- Usurped an unnamed god during the Age of Hubris.
- Created [[Corvel#Threadcleaver|Threadcleaver]] and gave it to an agent of hers operating on the Material Plane.
- The agent was eventually killed by a worshipper of the unnamed god and [[Corvel#Threadcleaver|Threadcleaver]] was returned to the Queen.
- It was then given to [[Corvel]] to serve him during his time on the Material Plane.
- Shortly thereafter it was stolen from him by [[Paraceleus]].
- Gave a vision to [[Corvel]] after he entered a pool of blood in a dungeon.
