Originally from a far-away land called Toronto, he was struck by a self-propelled carriage and awoke somewhere in [[Feyrndel]]. Some time after this, he joined the [[Player Party]] in their adventures.
# Memories of Another Land
While Leone generally speaks fondly of his homeland, his tone changes in a financial institution. He begins to rant about a cataclysmic event he calls "The Great Recession" wherein he claims banks misused customer funds to gamble on homeowners making mortgage payments, and when they failed the economy collapsed causing widespread panic, starvation and job loss. While this sounds completely fantastical, he insists he speaks the truth and is highly critical of banks in our world. His mistrust is so extreme that his bank account is merely months old and he refuses to place more than 10 gold in any one account.
