A group of 5 protectors charged with safeguarding the city of [[Skalos#Capital City (Kavyris)|Kavyris]] in [[Skalos]], [[Arkantos]].
Each Fang is bound to a dragon to aid them in protecting the city.
They act as an elite special forces unit for the Arkantos Military while ensuring there are no fewer than one Fang in Kavyris at any time.
