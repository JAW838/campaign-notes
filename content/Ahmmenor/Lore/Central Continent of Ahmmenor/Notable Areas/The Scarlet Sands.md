# Sunken City
An ancient metal city lies half-buried beneath the dunes of the Sands. Its name was Elvish, translating to "Wheel of Iron". Before [[The Shattering]], it was the sister city to [[Batel#The Big Hole|Adendia]], and floated about the world. During the catastrophe, the city fell to the ground in the Sands and has been buried by the sand in the time since. Nearby is a [[Tech Hunters|Tech Hunter]] base of operations.
