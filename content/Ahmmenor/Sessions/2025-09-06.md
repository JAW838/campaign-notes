# Notes
- Came across an abandoned campsite on the side of the mountain on our way to [[Batel#The Big Tower|The Big Tower]]. Someone had stayed here a day or two ago. Rain has destroyed other evidence.
- Beyond the campsite, found a crack in the mountainside which is actually a secret passage into the tower. Around it are ruins.
- The passage leads to several large chambers with moon-related puzzles in them. We solved the first puzzle so quickly, mr dm spent the next several minutes searching for harder ones.
- The chambers have remained untouched for who knows how long. 13 elven ghosts wander these passages and watch the [[Player Party]] as they traverse the tunnels. 13 is a scooby-doo reference
- Spoke to a 1500 year old elf ghost mage in the tunnels. He said he had been killed during [[The Shattering]] while performing his research. As he left, he said: "I'm leaving now, it's coming to get me again. It's so hard to think here. Leave before-"
- [[Richard]] failed a puzzle and summoned a vampire which was crushed by the party almost immediately.
- [[Ondo]] celebrates the victory by chugging an entire bottle of fine wine (10 servings) in 6 seconds, getting pretty drunk. [[Mike O'Flora]] casts Greater Restoration on him, cancelling his drunkenness.
- In the last chamber, they fought a wisp boss thing. It died in <2 rounds.
- Killing it opened a path to the observatory proper. End of session.
- Failing wisdom checks in the tunnels seems to make you fade away? Six phases, one for each failure.
1. Seemingly nothing happens at this level.
2. Disadvantage on perception checks, .
3. advantage on stealth checks
4. You can see the ghosts (see into the ethereal plane)
5. All of your attacks do half damage.
6. You are pushed into the ethereal plane and trapped in the catacombs, turning you into a ghost and trapping you there, effectively killing the character.
