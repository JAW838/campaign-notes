# Notes
## Disdain
- [[Player Party]] is on their way to [[Valorya#Disdain|Disdain]], an island in [[Valorya#Islands of Murder|Murder]] off the west coast of [[Valorya]].
- 12 year old kid arrives with a man dressed like a pirate greet them when they land and take them inside the castle.
- Inside the castle, [[Corvel]] goes to the chapel to pay his respects to the [[Raven Queen]].
- [[Leone]] has a private discussion with the leader of the Hunters in his office.
- Outside the office [[Ondo]] plays the Benny Hill theme on his new harmonica while [[Richard]] encourages him.
- [[Reen Voidswift]] and [[Diabel]] stay in the airship because the former is wanted by the [[Blood Hunters]] but are discovered during their routine safety check of visiting craft. [[Diabel]] casts Greater Invisibility on [[Reen Voidswift]] but it expires before the search party leaves. This leads to a Benny Hill-style chase scene between him and ~6 Hunters over the course of several minutes. During the chase he is hit by a Hunter attack allowing them to easily track him wherever he goes.
- He escapes for a time and hides in an unoccupied cave but is quickly discovered. He is taken to the mess hall, where he is confronted by a person. [[Diabel]] acts as his lawyer and they discover he has a special ability given to him by the Order and the [[Raven Queen]]. He deserted the Order and is sentenced to death.
- On hearing this, [[Ondo]] makes dumb comments and his mouth is divinely sealed. He retaliates by hitting the griddy and is subsequently tackled by [[Corvel]].
- As penance, [[Reen Voidswift]] is offered a path to redemption. All he must do is slay a specific creature on the island of [[Valorya#Cruelty|Cruelty]]. The entire [[Player Party]] volunteers to go with him, which was arguably not a good idea.
- Before they leave for the island their blood is collected by the Hunters. [[Richard]] also bets all of his money and wins 800 gold.
## Cruelty
- [[Player Party]] is deposited in the water just off the shore of [[Valorya#Cruelty|Cruelty]]. They swim to shore and head inland in search of their prey.
- They come across a fiend on their path. It talks to them and convinces them to let it travel with them for a bit, but the party quickly turns on it and kills it. In a matter of days it will return, remade from its skull, so they choose to take the head with them.
- For some reason they choose to follow [[Richard]] who immediately takes them towards the mountain in the centre of the island, the most dangerous part of it. They find a trail of blood and deer velvet, but it is the wrong time of year for deer to shed their velvet. They also come across strange claw and hoof marks.
- They decide to run away, and successfully find a cave to take shelter in overnight. However, during their watch they are attacked by a group of wolf-like creatures.
- Most of the wolves are defeated, but [[Reen Voidswift]] is captured by the only surviving wolf.
# Current Goals
- Save [[Reen Voidswift]] from the wolf-creature and kill the designated creature to escape the island and redeem him to the [[Blood Hunters]].
- Arrive at [[The Nimyren Empire#Narmor|Narmor]] and be hired by the government to hunt down the remaining [[Tech Hunters]] and acquire pardons for past crimes.
- Find [[Theofrastus Bombastus]] (potentially related to above).
- Go on [[Ondo]]'s vision quest.
