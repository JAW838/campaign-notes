# Pre-Session Notes
- Between sessions there was a discussion about the threat posed by the marks on [[Diabel Songlark]]'s back. [[Corvel]] suggested killing him, since the mark could wipe out the entire team at any time. However the participants of the discussion came to the conclusion the mark was not immediately dangerous and thus nothing came of the discussion.
# Notes
- [[Roving Seven]] returns to [[Valorya#Disdain|Disdain]] after their adventure on [[Valorya#Cruelty|Cruelty]].
- [[Reen Voidswift]] presents the antlered head of her quarry to the leaders of the [[Blood Hunters]] to a shocked reception. The leader of the [[Blood Hunters#Order of the Profane Soul|Profane Souls]] delivers an official pardon and accepts her back into the Order.
- [[Leon Redbrand]] goes off on his own to have a conversation with several high-up members of the [[Blood Hunters]], including the leader of the [[Blood Hunters#Order of the Mutant|Order of the Mutants]].
- There is a party after, celebrating Reen's return to the fold. During it, [[Ondo Edafoss]] engages in a 1v3 drinking competition against two elves and a human and loses to the human. After the party, the group heads off for [[The Nimyren Empire]] in their loaned airship.
- On their way, [[Magic Marty]] meets with them in his brand new [[Magic Marty#Magic Marty's Magical Moving Mart|Magic Marty's Magical Moving Mart]] to sell them magic items.
- After meeting Marty, the fiend skull in [[Reen Voidswift|Reen's]] backpack began to regenerate. Not wanting to deal with this enemy, the party decided to toss it overboard.
## Nimyren Empire
- The party flies over the walls of the capital city which have recently seen battle and fly straight to the central castle, like a medieval skyscraper. [[Sylas Blackstone|Sylas]] greets the party when they land.
- [[Diabel Songlark|Diabel]] receives his official pardon.
- The party dresses in nice, formal clothes to attend the negotiations for their quest to eradicate the [[Tech Hunters]]. Several dignitaries attend this meeting, including representatives from each province, which takes place in a large dining hall.
- The meeting begins with food and introductions, where the party impresses the group with their variety of abilities, skills, and items.
- Midway through the meeting, [[Emperor Ballas]] himself arrives. He leans on his staff and his steps are clearly pained but he carries himself with confidence.
- He is here to read the party for some purpose. The technology we are tasked with acquiring from the [[Tech Hunters]] must be quite valuable.
