# Notes
## Narmor
- [[Roving Seven]] prepares to depart [[The Nimyren Empire#Narmor|Narmor]].  They do some shopping, picking up various essential items and trinkets before their journey.
- [[Bartel B.]] decides to stay.
- They are being transported to the edge of [[The Scarlet Sands]] by the Nimyren military, and are travelling with some venerated soldiers.
## Scarlet Sands
- The airship lands just outside the eastern border of the [[The Scarlet Sands]] at the southern tip, in a town filled with Yuan-Ti and which sells lots of tools and other gear suited for crossing the desert.
- [[Roving Seven]] get four sand sleds to share between the seven of them.
- As they travel, they see two large silhouettes flying in the storm. A red and green dragon descend on the party, sporting armor and riders.
- These were two of [[Arkantos]]'s [[Arkantos#Five Fangs|Five Fangs]]. Since the party defeated one previously, there are only two remaining. These ones did not have a grudge against the party, so they were there either because of the contract or because they were working for someone who DID have a grudge.
- After a grueling fight, the dragons and their riders are defeated.
- The party immediately gets lost in the desert. They wander through the desolate wastes as they try to find their way.
- Two beings wearing long robes of snake skin appear from the dust, beckoning the party to follow.
- [[Diabel Songlark]] describes the party's quarry to the orcs, and they warn him to stay away from the [[The Scarlet Sands#Sunken City|Wheel of Iron]].
- They reveal they are the next test in [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]]'s trial, and the Circle of Bone is nearby for him to continue his quest.
# Current Goals

- Go on [[Ondo Edafoss]]'s vision quest.
