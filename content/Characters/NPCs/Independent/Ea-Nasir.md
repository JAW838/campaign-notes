Canon in all worlds, Ea-Nasir is a Fey Lord famous for selling shitty copper.
# Real Life
Ea-Nasir (more accurately [Ea-nāṣir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ea-n%C4%81%E1%B9%A3ir)) was a Mesopotamian copper merchant. He received some of the earliest known hate mail regarding the low quality copper he provided to a client.
