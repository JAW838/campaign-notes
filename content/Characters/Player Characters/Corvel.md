# Threadcleaver
## General
Corvel's signature weapon, Threadcleaver is a scythe with a sharp obsidian stem and a raven-like skull apparatus connecting the blade to the stem. The blade protrudes from the skull like a beak and its eyes glow when the weapon flies about, attacking at the heed of its wielder.
## Artifact Weakness
As an [[Artifacts|Artifact]], Threadcleaver can be destroyed only by the utterance of the name of the god usurped by the [[Raven Queen]] in its presence.
# Bongos Mentorship
During his travels, Corvel came across a remote band of hermits with a peculiar knack for music. They spent their days farming and herding their cattle, and during the evening they would gather around a bonfire and sing. Children of the hermits could not participate in the days activities and so would be kept in a daycare of sorts, teaching them life skills, history, and music. As he stayed there, he grew deeply intwined with the people and eventually found himself aiding in the care of the children, where he found a strange passion for teaching the younglings the music of the bongo. For a while, he was happy.
But nothing lasts forever. After a particularly extravagant evening of comradery, in the deepest depths of the night, a wicked sorcerer attacked the camp and killed indiscriminately. Not just the men, but the women and the children were mutilated and turned into undead soldiers serving only evil. Corvel did what was expected of him. As the sun lit the sky a beautiful orange, Corvel left the ashes of the camp and continued his journey, his commitment burning anew but his soul drowned in sadness.
# Events
- Threadcleaver was lost to Paraceleus in a battle about 200 years ago, shortly after Corvel had arrived on the Material Plane. He has been looking for it since, with little success.
- After killing a necromancer in a village centre, he was imprisoned. The party eventually freed him, along with [[Leon Redbrand]]'s brother and [[Sylas Blackstone]]. He then joined the party on their adventures after receiving a sign from a raven.
- Had a vision of [[Paraceleus]] holding Threadcleaver in a sandstorm and the sand was blood red (likely [[The Scarlet Sands]]).
- During the [[Seige on The Big Tower]] ([[2025-11-15]]), Corvel's right hand was severed by an enemy Red Slaad. In an attempt to preserve it, [[Mike O'Flora]] reanimated it, turning it into his servant.
- After the [[Seige on The Big Tower]], Corvel received Threadcleaver from [[Paraceleus]] in exchange for letting him and his remaining [[Tech Hunters]] go. He donated his previous scythe to a local farmer.
