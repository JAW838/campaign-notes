A Fey gambling game where the last player standing wins.
# Gameplay
The players sit around a table with a deck of 66 cards in the middle. The cards are inspired by the Deck of Many Things which the game is named after. Each round the players place an ever-increasing amount of money into the pot and draw a card which has a Jumanji-like effect. A player is out if they run out of money to put in the pot or are unable to stay at the table. The winner gets the pot.
## Buy-In
During the first round, each player places one gold in the pot on their turn. In the second round, they place two, and so on.
# Cards
32 - map: gives knowledge of the next 5 cards on the deck
ruin: all your money is placed in the pile
cave: places the players in a cave
