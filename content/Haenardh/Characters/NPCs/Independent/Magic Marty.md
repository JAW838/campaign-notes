A recurring NPC who shows up all around the world. His shop, Magic Marty's Magic Mart, sells discount magic items with potentially harmful or otherwise unhelpful side effects.
# Businesses
## Magic Marty's Magic Mart
Marty's primary business venture, the Magic Mart is a magic items shop which moves from place to place.
## Magic Marty's Magical Moving Mart
Shortly before [[2026-01-31]], Marty acquired a hot air balloon, which he modified and transformed into a Magical Moving Mart. During [[2026-01-31]], he docked the Moving Mart with the [[Roving Seven]]'s loaned airship.
