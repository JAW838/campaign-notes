Eldest of the Land, this province was made from the remnants of the old Elvish Territories and is the centre of the 3 greater provinces.

Ruled by [[Levitas|Levitas]], the 4th king of the elves. His line is said to descend from the old world & Levitas himself was the King to agree to [[Emperor Ballas|Ballas]]’ Rule when the Empire was founded. He is aided in his rule by a small [[Levitas#Councilors|council containing 5 esteemed members of Elvish Nobility]] as well as his [[Levitas#Royal Liaison|Royal Liaison]] & Right Hand [[Levitas#Dezeran Thain|Dezeran Thain]].
# Elven Kings of Gulfen
As of present, there have been four Kings of Gulfen:
- [[Erlking|The Erlking]], the First Elven King. Arrived from lands unknown to establish Gulfen as one of the world's earliest societies and his [[Gulfen#The Alabaster Throne|Alabaster Throne]] as an important authority within the world at large.
- [[Neurodorin|King Neurodorin]], the Second Elven King. Led Gulfen through [[The World#The War of Divine Egress|The War of Divine Egress]] and was responsible for repairing some of the damage of the War and slaying many Great Beasts of Myth.
- [[Belodrin|King Belodrin the Baned]], the Third Elven King. Responsible for the establishment of several metropolises and the advancement of magic, and known for being driven by his fear of the curse on his throne.
- [[Levitas|King Levitas]], the Fourth Elven King. The current ruler and responsible for Gulfen's joining of [[The Nimyren Empire]]. He has successfully navigated several bloody conflicts, though nothing as extreme as his predecessors.
## The Alabaster Throne
The throne occupied by the King of Gulfen. It is believed to have been cursed by the god that was responsible for the death of the [[Erlking]]: the reign of each King will be shorter than the last. This curse played a large role in the actions of [[Belodrin|Belodrin the Baned]].
