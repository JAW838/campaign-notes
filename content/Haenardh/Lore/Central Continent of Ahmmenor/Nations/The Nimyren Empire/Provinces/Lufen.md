Northernmost Province, Formed from the greater lands of the old Dwarvish Kingdom and is the northernmost of the 3 greater provinces.

After the death of its ruler shortly after the founding of the Empire a brief war of succession commenced, ending with the 5 most prominent lords of the land each being named “King” of their respective territories & the one among them with the best.
# The Wall
A more than 2000 mile long, 10 story tall structure that spans the length of the flatlands between [[The North]]’s Eastern & Western mountains.
# The 5 Kings of Lufen
- [[Durin Ballinor]], King of Pharûnar & Bearer of the Mithril Crown
- [[Thráin Bahammar]], King of Arulain & Bearer of the Opulent Crown
- [[Brienne Stoneheart]], Queen of Runestone & Bearer of the Crown of Stones
- [[Eirwin Sormbreaker]], King of Winterhome AKA The Crownless King
- [[Thorin Brazik]], High King of Sundari & Bearer of the Forge Keeper’s Crown
# Major Cities
## Pharûnar
Ruled by [[Durin Ballinor]], the city is the source of a third of the continent's Mithril. A place of great import and much pride within Lufen.
## Arulain
Ruled by [[Thráin Bahammar]] and greatest in wealth and vanity, here is where the [[The Nimyren Empire|Empire]] mines most of its typical precious metals & mints its currency. It is also home to a massive, impenetrable vault.
## Runestone
Ruled by [[Brienne Stoneheart]], it is a city built into the vast ruins of a Giant city that once held sway over the land millennia ago, the people of this place have learned from their predecessors & have put their magic of runes & enchantment to good use. It is also home to great stoneworkers, many of whom are tasked with upkeep upon the wall.
## Winterhome
Ruled by [[Eirwin Sormbreaker]], Winterhome is the city of frost, as it faces the north head on. Its soldiers serve as the wardens of the wall & guard the main entrance to the Duergar city of Lu’Duerra.
## Sundari
Ruled by [[Thorin Brazik]], Spiritual Capitol to the Dwarves of the empire & constructed in honor of The Mordinsamman, the city is home to “The Eternal Flame” a forge said to have been set alight prior to [[The World#The Dawn War|The Dawn War]], & by the All-Hammer himself. The ceiling of the subterranean city is held up by 7 massive statues of ancient clan leaders. The city contains temples to all the gods of the Dwarven pantheon, apart from Abbathor the god of Greed.
