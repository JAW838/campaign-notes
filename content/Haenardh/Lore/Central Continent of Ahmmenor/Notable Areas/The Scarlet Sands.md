# Angruin
The ancient metal city of Angruin lies half-buried beneath the dunes of the Sands. Its name was Elvish, translating to "Wheel of Iron". Before [[The Shattering]], it was the sister city to [[Batel#The Big Hole|Adendia]], and floated about the world. During the catastrophe, the city fell to the ground in the Sands and has been buried by the sand in the time since. 
## Angruin History
Many mysteries remain regarding the construction of Angruin. When it was constructed, how long it took, why it was constructed, and even where it was constructed are unknown. However, it is known that the city lifted into the sky ~1000 years before it fell out of the sky, landing in the Sands.
### Destinations
During its time in the air, Angruin roamed the plane, visiting many different places. Exactly where it went is unknown.
### How It Worked
Other floating cities of the time, including [[Batel#The Big Hole|Adendia]], were held aloft either by a one powerful magic spell or several smaller spells of the same function. Keeping a city in the sky is no small feat, and several thousand spellcasters were required to keep it there at any given time. Angruin was intended to circumvent these requirements, and instead used 9 primary arcane generating sites to generate powerful fields which kept the city afloat. Being highly automated, the thousands of spellcasters were not required to keep Angruin in the sky, instead it could be operated by a much smaller number of people. Its reliance on non-magical propulsion is likely the primary reason the city and all on it were not immediately killed during [[The Shattering]].
### The Shattering
Angruin briefly stayed airborne after [[The Shattering]], but was quickly landed due to strain on flight mechanics. The landing caused minimal damage to the city and its flight apparatus. It was intentionally landed in the Scarlet Sands to soften the landing for its inhabitants.

The city remained inhabited for several decades after the cataclysm. There was an attempt to restore the city to its former glory and roam the skies once more but this effort failed due to lack of population. After ~30 years of being stranded in the desert, the city was completely abandoned. Specifics about its abandonment remain a mystery, however a being which petrified dozens of citizens into metal statues may have been involved.

## Analytical Archive
Housed in a building reminiscent of a large library, the Archive is a machine created for storage and analysis of data, record keeping, and more. Of the several Analytical devices littered around the city, the Archive serves as a central hub, containing the most information.
### Archive History
The Analytical Archive was originally constructed 800 years before Angruin lifted off. It functioned for those 800 years and survived the city's descent. Between then and the city's abandonment ~30 years later, various updates and upgrades were applied to it.
