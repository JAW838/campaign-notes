# Angruin
The ancient metal city of Angruin lies half-buried beneath the dunes of the Sands. Its name was Elvish, translating to "Wheel of Iron". Before [[The Shattering]], it was the sister city to [[Batel#The Big Hole|Adendia]], and floated about the world. During the catastrophe, the city fell to the ground in the Sands and has been buried by the sand in the time since. Now, the city is like a military camp or fortress, with watch towers, spotlights, and smoke from factories and laboratories inside. A giant robot guards the city. Nearby is a [[Tech Hunters|Tech Hunter]] base of operations.
