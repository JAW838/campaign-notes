# History
The Mark 5 Analytical Engine was constructed shortly after the beginning of [[The Shattering]] and used as a clerical device for a shop in [[The Scarlet Sands#Angruin|Angruin]] until ~30 years after the event. Presumably the city was abandoned around this time due to its falling out of the sky and general chaos caused by [[The Shattering]].
# Abilities
The Engine requires an authentication card to use, which resembles a punch card. It takes input from a typewriter-like assembly, and gives responses by printing its words on paper. It responds best to questions posed in full sentences, such as "When were you constructed." It can also print maps quests (instructions to places).
