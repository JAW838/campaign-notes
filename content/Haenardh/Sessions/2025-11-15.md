# Notes
- Several enemies surround the [[Roving Seven]], including a Kolyarut, a Red Slaad and six gnomes.
- During the fight, [[Corvel]] hit the Red Slaad with a particularly damaging attack (doing 61 damage over 2 attacks), the Red Slaad retaliated with a precise attack against [[Corvel]]'s right hand, removing it entirely.
- [[Leon Redbrand]] lost his left hand as he attempted to retreat from his melee with the Red Slaad as it slashed out with its sword hands. He reattached his hand with the help of [[Mike O'Flora]].
- Arriving at the top of the mountain the party finds an observatory containing [[Paraceleus]], several other members of the [[Tech Hunters]], and [[Diabel Songlark]], bloodied and in chains.
- [[Paraceleus]] offers the [[Roving Seven]] a deal: in exchange for letting the remaining members of the [[Tech Hunters]] go, they will receive [[Diabel Songlark]] and [[Corvel#Threadcleaver|Threadcleaver]]. After a 6-1 vote, the party accepts the deal and saves [[Diabel Songlark]].
- [[Bartel B.]] sneaks a tracking ring onto [[Theofrastus Bombastus]], allowing the [[Roving Seven]] to track him.
- During the confrontation, [[Leon Redbrand|Leon]] sneaks through a [[Tech Hunters|Tech Hunter]] portal in the room and arrives in their base of operations, where he has a conversation with their leader.
- [[Mike O'Flora]] stays in [[Batel#The Big Tower|The Big Tower]], adopting it as a base of operations and transforming it into a tree-like home.
- The [[Roving Seven]] effects basic repairs on an airship left at the [[Batel#The Big Tower|Tower]] and ride it down to the base of the mountain.
