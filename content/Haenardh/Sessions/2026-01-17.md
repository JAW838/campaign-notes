# Notes
- [[Roving Seven]] follows the beast that took [[Reen Voidswift]] to [[Valorya#The Hollow|The Hollow]]. Screeches emanate from the area, but it is impossible to tell whether the screams are [[Reen Voidswift|Reen]] or mimicry.
- [[Leon Redbrand]] goes ahead into [[Valorya#The Hollow|The Hollow]] using his stealth and discovers an ambush laid by monsters, and the screams are mimicry to lure the party to their demise.
- The party engage the monsters that laid the trap and dispatch several.
- Ice blocks the way forward but [[Ondo Edafoss]] melts it and hits the griddy.
- As the party enters the next area, they hear a cacophony of laughter as a blizzard obscures their vision. [[Reen Voidswift|Reen]] is tied up near a frozen lake.
- On their way to free her, they are ambushed by more monsters along with Boreas, the monster the party came to kill and the one that kidnapped [[Reen Voidswift|Reen]].
- As the fight rapidly devolved into a struggle for survival, [[Leon Redbrand|Leon]] cashed in a favour. A strange black tendril monster emerged from a coin he pulled from his pocket and rescued the party by killing Boreas. The monster had a strange symbol instead of a face. It then went back into the coin and back into Leons pocket.
- [[Richard]] saw the great Ted in the sky and the "Tedster Life".
- The party returned to the coastline and rendezvoused with the ferryman who was not expecting the party to live, let alone succeed in their mission.
