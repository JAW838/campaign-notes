# Notes
- [[Roving Seven]] come across a huge skull made of black stone. Mountains in the distance are part of its spine. Numerous bonfires have been lit in its segmented jaws. Orcs, elves, and teiflings emerge from tents wearing cloaks of white snake scale.
- An elder approaches the party and has a conversation with [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]], explaining what the [[The Scarlet Sands#Circle of Bone|Circle of Bone]] is. He tasks [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]] with casting one of their spells and surviving.
- He leads the party underground, across a bridge hanging over lava, and takes them to an altar with a scroll on it. The scroll is written an Abyssal and despite his being unable to read it, Ondo attempts to cast the spell. The party does what they can to help but Ondo fails, the bridge is destroyed and a large monster appears.
- The following fight was quite difficult, with multiple downed party members.
- The bridge is reconstructed and the party is led into a tunnel of obsidian leading to a temple chamber. In the centre is a large iron obelisk. Skeletal creatures seem to cling to its edges. Many scrolls line the walls, and these are the Thousand Ancient Scrolls.
- As [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]] touches the obelisk, he succumbs to a vision. A figure dressed in black-red armor with a skull-like face and antlers appears, with seven eyes across his chest. He offers Ondo immense power. Ondo does not engage with the being, and it gets bored and leaves.
- A mural on the wall depicts The Changeling, a creature summoned by [[Leon Redbrand]] during [[2026-01-17]]. It is an ancient being from the Old World: a Primordial.
- Despite the obelisk clearly being bad news, [[Richard]] and [[Reen Voidswift]] decide to touch it. Several party members grab them and pull them away, leaving the pair with skeleton-shaped burn marks and scars.
- After shenanigans, the party is kicked out of the temple. [[Leon Redbrand]] and [[Leone]] use Dimension Door to get back into the temple where they make contact with the obelisk. They see a throne with The Changeling on it. [[Leone]] is quickly rejected by the Changeling, but [[Leon Redbrand|Leon]] stays for a longer conversation. A bone devil is also present.
- Leon is nearly sucked into the obelisk, but some guards barely manage to pull him out. All party members except [[Ondo Edafoss]] and [[Corvel]] are locked in a cell overnight, to be released in the morning.
