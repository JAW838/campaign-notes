# Notes
- [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]] speaks to the village elder Draca to tells him that his next trial is the air trial at the [[Sea of Storm]].
- The [[Roving Seven]] are escorted out of the camp by armed guards. They begin their trek across [[The Scarlet Sands]].
- While travelling they come across some corpses with they loot for gold and very old wine.
- They approach [[The Scarlet Sands#Angruin|Angruin]], the Wheel of Iron. A scouting party consisting of [[Leif Vornsen]] and [[Leon Redbrand]] stealthily enter the city to investigate.
- What they find is that the area is essentially deserted. There are no people in the residential area or in the market. 
- Nearby they see a tower/laboratory with electrical coils and people moving in and out. The pair peer into a window and spot [[Theofrastus Bombastus]] and [[Paraceleus]] poring over a document and debating something. In a stroke of bad luck, one of the Hunters looks out the window and spots [[Leif Vornsen|Diabel]]. [[Leon Redbrand|Leon]] quickly teleports into the building, grabs a map of the city, and teleports out before running. The pair split up and Leon looses his pursuers, the remaining ones follow Diabel.
- Diabel summons a dragon to fly away but spots a demonic motorcycle and decides he wants it. He swoops down and hops on the motorcycle, starting an extended chase scene.
- He gets out of city but finds himself chased by the large guard. The bike is hit by a blast from the guard and explodes violently. Badly wounded, Diabel summons his dragon once again and attempts to flee. The dragon is struck by another blast, and Diabel flies it up and away from danger.
- The map reveals that [[Gestalt]] and [[Dee]] are also in the city, though in different areas.
- [[Richard]] has a schizophrenic episode and attacks a twig in a cave. The twig explodes in green flame, and is reduced to ashes.
- From there, the party begins to concoct a plan to assault the city with the goals of:
1. Killing [[Theofrastus Bombastus]], [[Paraceleus]], [[Gestalt]], [[Dee]], and as many other enemy forces as possible.
2. Destroying remaining [[Tech Hunters|Tech Hunter]] infrastructure.
3. Stealing whatever technology they can.
## The Plan
1. Lure the Warforged Colossus out into the desert and trick it into falling into a large hole. Enter the Colossus and kill its inhabitants and attempt to commandeer it. If the commandeering is successful, do step 2. otherwise skip to step 3.
2. Bring it to the city and do as much damage with it as possible. That includes attempting to destroy the factory in the centre of the city. Perform step 3 at the same time. 
3. Kill as many soldiers on bikes as possible on the way to the next target.
4. Kill [[Dee]].
5. Kill [[Gestalt]].
6. Kill [[Paraceleus]] and [[Theofrastus Bombastus]].
# Goals
- Complete [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]]'s air trial at the [[Sea of Storm]].
- Hunt down and destroy the remaining members of the [[Tech Hunters]].
