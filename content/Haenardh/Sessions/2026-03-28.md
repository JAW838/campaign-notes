# Notes
- [[Debonesby]] commits mail fraud by forging a package sent by [[Leif Vornsen]] and addressed to him and returning it, thereby charging Diabel for shipping. By doing this, he mailed himself to the party's current location in [[The Scarlet Sands]] to sell them products.
- He trades a powerful sword to [[Leon Redbrand]], and in exchange receives blood from all party members.
- He then leaves.
- After doing business, the [[Roving Seven]] excavate a large area to trap the Warforged Colossus and use a sandstorm to draw it away from the nearby city. They succeed in trapping it and boarding it.
- After a hard fight, the party is forced to abandon their plan of capturing the mech when they are unable to stop the self-destruct sequence. However, they discover an array of escape motorcycles located in the abdomen and ride them out of the mech as it detonates.
- As the saying goes, the party has jumped from the frying pan and into the fire as they hear the drone of motorcycles coming closer.
- At the same time, the city begins to rumble and shake. It is beginning to, in the DM's words, "take off". The party quickly turns their motorcycles towards it and, as the enemy cycles close in, barely make it onto the city as it rises into the air.
# Goals
- Complete [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]]'s air trial at the [[Sea of Storm]].
- Hunt down and destroy the remaining members of the [[Tech Hunters]].
