# Notes
- First target in the newly-floating city: [[Dee]].
- As the city rises through the sandstorm, the party gets lost in the low visibility of the city. On top of that, [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]] and [[Diabel Songlark|Diabel]] get separated from the party, becoming double lost.
- The remaining party comes across an old dilapidated workshop with a staircase to the second floor. At this point they realise two party members are missing. The steampunk-esque tech is strewn around the room, along with pieces of metal and glass. On one wall is a typewriter-type machine with a large lever. [[Richard]] types "Ted" into the machine and pulls the lever, awakening a computer-like machine which requests authentication. Behind the machine is a dead body holding a punch card which the machine accepts. It is a Mark 5 Analytical Engine, created for storage and analysis of data, record keeping. After extensive questioning, it gives the location of the Analytical Archive located elsewhere in the city. The shop owner has a single orc-elf yaoi novel.
- Ondo and Diabel stumble down a hole into a subway tunnel and come face to face with statues with faces frozen with fear. The statues are a rusty metal and covered in pitting and holes going straight through them. Diabel is unable to stop Ondo from going down the tunnel, and they follow it until they come across a subway station with a map.
- The map marks several key areas around the city, including the locations of the party's targets. It also marks the location of the Analytical Archive, where Diabel decides to go to gather information and do more research.
- At the same time, the party acquires a map to the Archive from the Engine.
- Underground, the partners come across a derailed train car which has been partially torn apart. Frozen metal statues are locked in time, fleeing the scene, their faces filled with fear.
- The above ground group arrives at the Analytical Archive first. It looks like a massive library. There are a couple people on patrol but the area is mostly empty.
- The [[Roving Seven]] creates a list of questions to ask the Archive, then send in [[Bartel B.]], who has turned invisible, to submit them to the Archive. The questions and their answers can be found below.
- They then head toward [[Dee]]. On the way they come across more and more strange metal statues. On closer inspection, [[Diabel Songlark]] discovers they bear the marks of being disenchanted, indicating these are people turned to metal and not sculptures.
- [[Dee]] is in a clock tower.
