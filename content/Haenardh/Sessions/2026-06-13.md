# Notes
- The party arrives in front of a clockwork building with strange architecture. 3 large armoured guards are inside. With [[Corvel]]'s aid, [[Diabel Songlark|Diabel]] creates a convincing illusion which lures 2 of the guards away. The party rushes the remaining guard, quickly killing it. The guards immediately turn to chase the party but they step into the building, closing and barricading the door with a nearby desk.
- The party heads to the top of the building past the nice areas and into a more mechanical area filled with gears and mechanisms and contraptions none of the characters can comprehend which move the clock. Faced with the choice between moving up through the gears or up the stairs and facing more guards.
- [[Leone]] flies out a window and up to a balcony on the next floor. In the room beyond he sees an enemy standing on an impassable bridge. The other characters are able to climb the gears past the enemies on the stairs but will be unable to climb past this one. [[Leone]] flies into the enemy and barely manages to push them off the bridge into the pit below, killing them.
- The party moves up to the top floor, encountering a group of strong enemies along with [[Dee]]. After a tough fight, the enemies are defeated.
- The research being done here is reminiscent of what was done to the inhabitants of the city.
- A robot after the fight let out an attack similar to what was used on the inhabitants.
- A number of green flashes came from the central factory which temporarily dimmed most of the city. A larger flash occurred, and the lights went out. When they came on, more lights around the city came on than were on before. Additionally, after the lights came on a persistent humming from the centre of the city ceased.
- Now that the storm has subsided slightly, several faded propaganda posters are visible on several walls.
