# Notes
- After killing [[Dee]] and taking a long rest, the [[Roving Seven]] choose [[Paraceleus]] as their next target. He is near a place called the [[The Scarlet Sands#Aetherforge|Aetherforge]].
- [[Leon Redbrand]] returns from his independent scouting mission: the Aetherforge is fully operational. The good news is that there are 9 generators lining the edge of the city which provide power to the Forge. Disabling some of those generators would deprive the Aetherforge of power and thus shut it down, but it would also lessen the power flowing to the engines keeping the city in the sky.
- [[Gideon McEiben]] is using refurbished [[The Scarlet Sands#Angruin|Angruin]] robots to drain and collect magical energy from objects and people.
- After much deliberation, the Seven concoct the following plan:
  1. Strike one of the generation stations.
  2. Seeing their stations are vulnerable, the [[Tech Hunters]] would redeploy their forces to the remaining stations, leaving their target [[Paraceleus]] vulnerable.
  3. Find and kill Paraceleus.
- However they cannot decide on a method which is certain to disable the generators so they head to the nearest station to gather information and plan their next move.
- On their trip, [[Leif Vornsen]] and [[Leon Redbrand]] get into an argument, and a shadowy figure emerges from a coin posessed by Leon. It introduces itself as [[Ikol]], a changeling.
- [[Ikol]] makes a deal with [[Ondo Edafoss]], giving him candy in exchange for being his friend. He has generally unsettling talks with other members of the party before returning to the coin.
- The party continues to the generator, embarking on a stealth mission to gain more information about the facility and its operations. They decide to simultaneously deprive the burner of water and close all intake and output valves, starving the engine. 
# Goals
- Complete [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]]'s air trial at the [[Sea of Storm]].
- Hunt down and destroy the remaining members of the [[Tech Hunters]].
