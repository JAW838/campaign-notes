> _“There's always a bigger Tony.”_
# Notes
- [[Leif Vornsen]] begins the session by walking up to the front door of the generator station. Using minor illusion to project a mustache onto his face, he introduces himself as Inspector John Gadget to the guard at the front door. This nearly works, but the guard notices his mustache is not moving with his face. With his cover blown, [[Leif Vornsen]] attacks the guard, starting combat.
- As the [[Roving Seven]] attack the workers and destroy the burner, an Immolith emerges from the burner it was fueling. Several characters are downed in the ensuing fight, but the party is victorious.
- After some light looting, it is discovered that [[Theofrastus Bombastus]] was in the building, and retreated when the fighting started.
- Having accomplished their mission of destroying a generator station, they find a new hideout and take a short rest there.
# Goals
- Complete [[Ondo Edafoss|Ondo]]'s air trial at the [[Sea of Storm]].
- Hunt down and destroy the remaining members of the [[Tech Hunters]].
