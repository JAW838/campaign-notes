# Previous Arc
The [[Roving Seven]] participated in the [[Seige on The Big Tower]], resulting in the expulsion of the [[Tech Hunters]] from the [[Batel#The Big Tower|The Big Tower]]. After this, they travel to [[The Nimyren Empire#Narmor|Narmor]] to negotiate a contract with the Empire to hunt down the remaining Hunters and retrieve their research.
# The Hunt Begins
- [[2026-01-31]]: [[Roving Seven]] have a meeting with prominent leaders of [[The Nimyren Empire]] in its capital city of [[The Nimyren Empire#Narmor|Narmor]], where they sign a contract to exterminate the remaining [[Tech Hunters]].
- [[2026-02-07]]: [[Roving Seven]] leave [[The Nimyren Empire#Narmor|Narmor]] for [[The Scarlet Sands]] and begin their journey across the desert.
