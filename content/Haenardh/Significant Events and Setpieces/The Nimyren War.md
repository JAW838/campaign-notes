A war triggered shortly after the end of [[The Inquisition]], and caused by significant numbers of dwarves and elves climbing out of the woodworks and competing for power in what is now [[The Nimyren Empire]]. Its end resulted in the Empire's current borders and structure.
# Before the War
The Kingdom of the Elves and the Kingdom of the Dwarves hid themselves away to hide from the brutal ongoings of [[The Inquisition]]. They emerged after the fervor subsided to find their lands inhabited by a vast nation of Humanity. It was not long before war broke out.
# The War
Centuries of bitter fighting commenced between the Dwarves, Elves and Humans. Peace during this time was tenuous and short lived, and destruction ravaged the country.
# The End of the War
246 years ago, a strange figure approached the leaders of the 3 Prominent Factions. He convinced them to lay down their arms and set their differences aside for the common good. In their gratitude, the 3 Leaders granted this strange the title of Emperor of the newly united [[The Nimyren Empire]], giving rise to [[Emperor Ballas]], who holds power to this day.
