The central continent of the world, it is held in a delicate political balance between multiple prominent nations. The primary location of the current campaign.
# Nations
## Arkantos
A nation full of diversity, many people fled to [[Arkantos]] after the destruction wrought by [[The Shattering]]. Many there are fluent in the way of magic.
## Batel
Home of the Seven Wonders, [[Batel]] sports a mountain range and several scars left by The Shattering.
## The Nimyren Empire
The dominant force in the region, [[The Nimyren Empire]] is split into 5 provinces, each with a unique governing structure. It is the most warlike nation, having conquered its eastern neighbor not 10 years ago and currently engaged in [[The Nimyren Empire-Arkantos War]].
## Valorya
Home to a large floating city, home to the headquarters of the [[Monster Hunters]].
## Leore
Sandwiched between The Nimyren Empire and [[Valorya]] and sitting on two major trade routes, [[Leore]] boasts a strong economy which makes it the envy of many.  It is composed mainly of farmland with marshland to the southwest.
