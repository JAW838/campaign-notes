Sandwiched between [[The Nimyren Empire]] and [[Valorya]] & located on the intersection of two major trade routes, this small square nation (roughly 150 miles across) is an important center for commerce & the object of desire for its two large neighbors. Mostly farmland with only one major city & an area of marshland to the southwest to break up the plains.
# Ruling Factions
## The Noble Council
The Noble Council, contrary to its name, is largely made up of elected officials from the various Hamlets & Villages that pepper the countryside.  
## The Trader's Guild
The Trader’s Guild, a world spanning group of wealthy merchants with a vested interest in this trade hub.  
## Stovar Talsain
[[Stovar Talsain]], an important member of both the Noble Council & the Trader’s Guild, this Dwarf resides in the Fools Crossing and owns approximately 35% of the land in the nation. He is also the main funder of the land’s “Military”, whose function is mostly just to act as guards in settlements & provide added protection to trade caravans. Originally from [[Lufen#Major Cities#Arulain|Arulain]].
# Capital City (Fools Crossing)
This walled city is situated at the center of the nation & rests upon the trade route intersection.
## Notable Locations
- The Council Building, where the Noble Council go’s to hold meetings & pass laws.  
- The Guild hall, where applications for the setting up of merchant stalls can be submitted & records of all legal trade conducted in the city are kept.  
- The major Marketplace / Plazas.  
- The Griffon Market, Where General goods are sold  
- The Dragon Market, Where Goods of a more magical nature are sold (Located next to the guild hall)  
- The Unicorn Market, Where Specialty Goods are sold  
- The Chimera Market / Night Market which has no set location & deals in illegal goods  
## Other Notable Locations and People
- The 5 Guard Posts around the city as well as the Guard Capitan Garry  
- The Toothless Orc, a inn & tavern run by Aldo, an Orc with a mimic for a tongue
