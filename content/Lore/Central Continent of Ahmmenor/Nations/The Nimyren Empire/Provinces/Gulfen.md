Eldest of the Land, this province was made from the remnants of the old Elvish Territories and is the centre of the 3 greater provinces.

Ruled by [[King Levitas of Gulfen|King Levitas]], the 4th king of the elves. His line is said to descend from the old world & Levitas himself was the King to agree to [[Emperor Ballas|Ballas]]’ Rule when the Empire was founded. He is aided in his rule by a small [[King Levitas of Gulfen#Councilors|council containing 5 esteemed members of Elvish Nobility]] as well as his [[King Levitas of Gulfen#Royal Liaison|Royal Liaison]] & Right Hand [[King Levitas of Gulfen#Dezeran Thain|Dezeran Thain]].
# Capital City (Valkor)
Home to much of the Provinces Nobility as well as the Illuminate Academy, & the lands seat of power A palace known as the Ivory Tower.
