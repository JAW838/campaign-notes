The World is covered by three main continents: the eastern continent of [[Feyrndel]], the central continent of [[Ahmmenor]], and the western continent of [[Drisalygg]] (not pictured).

Some time ago, [[The Shattering]] occurred, producing several new landmarks around the world.

# Timeline
Shortly after the beginning of creation, there was [[The Dawn War]], which some think colour [[The Scarlet Sands]] with the blood of the beings slain during it. After that, [[The Great Winter]] froze the world, and its end was a time of great prosperity(?). Some time after the Winter, [[The Shattering]]
wrought destruction upon the world, the likes of which had never been seen before or since. This cataclysmic event triggered several brutal conflicts in what is now [[The Nimyren Empire]].
