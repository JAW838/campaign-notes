## Samson
A ghost horse named Samson pulls the hearse. It is a real ghost and thus has unfinished business but nobody can talk to it or figure out what it needs to do. [[Karra]] realised its name after meeting it for the first time. Calling the horse "Gregor Samson" inflicts psychic damage on whomever made the comment.
