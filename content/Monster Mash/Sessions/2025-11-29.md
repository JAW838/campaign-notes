# Notes
- In the Outlands, near Tradegate
- [[Llort]] and [[Ergo]] are travelling together when they spot a hearse at the bottom of a cliff. They both fall off the cliff and [[Aurel]] and [[Karra]] watch from the carriage. The carriage crew decide the others could be useful in their ongoing hunt and so take them along.
- Down the road they come across some fiends, and they easily defeat the enemies. Having successfully hunted their quarry, [[Aurel]] and [[Karra]] decide to take [[Llort]] and [[Ergo]] decide to essentially adopt the others and bring them with them to the town to deliver proof of their hunt.
- The newly-formed [[Monster Party]] arrives in Tradegate to deliver the skull of the fiend to [[Lucky]] and are paid with 400gp, split evenly between the 4 characters. [[Ergo]] buys an insane amount of food, [[Llort]] buys a new weapon, and [[Aurel]] also buys food.
