# Notes
- [[Devalyn]] finds the [[Monster Party]] and solicits their help in protecting him during an anthropological expedition to Bytopia.
- A comment he made led to a long discussion about how time works in the Outer Planes.
- The party finds the Trademaster, who demands an item of equal value in exchange for travel to Bytopia. [[Llort]] exchanges his right arm, [[Ergo]] gives a "pet" rotting carcass, he took something from [[Karra]], [[Aurel]] gave up a chunk of metal, [[Devalyn]] gave him a donation box, and [[Monster Party#Samson|Samson]] locked eyes with the Trademaster who allowed him through. Presumably the horse gave something up, but what is unknown. After each exchange another character is transported to the first layer of Bytopia, Dothion, outside the Golden Hills.
- [[Monster Party]] stays with a local farmer overnight.
- The next day the party ascends a spire to the other side of Bytopia, crossing a toll bridge on the way up.
- At the halfway point, there is a 15 foot gap straight up. [[Karra]] and [[Devalyn]] fly up, and [[Monster Party#Samson|Samson]] uses his ghost powers to lift himself and the hearse to the other side. The others climb their way up.
- After surviving a rockslide, the party makes their way to Shurrock.
- They come across a group of hill giants and [[Ergo]] and [[Llort]] talk with them. It was a strange conversation.
- Notetaker zoned out but [[Aurel]] talked to some mages.
- They send the party to the market ward of Sigil where he buys lots of blood.
- From here the party splits: [[Devalyn]], [[Ergo]] and [[Llort]] go to the Hive Ward. They encounter a Bone Devil who takes them to a tavern to discuss evil things.
- [[Karra]] and [[Aurel]] go to the Hall of Information to find things on something [[Karra]] is looking for.
