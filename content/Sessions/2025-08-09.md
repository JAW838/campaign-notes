# Notes
- The party admired a hole torn in the fabric of reality left by [[The Shattering]] known as [[Batel#The Big Hole|The Big Hole]]. A creature climbed out which [[Corvel]] inspected, although it could not get past the barrier created by the series of columns placed around the hole.
- [[Leon]] had a conversation with [[Sylas]] where they made a deal: the party would extract research from an enemy facility in [[Batel#The Big Tower|The Big Tower]] as payment for the location of [[Paraceleus]]. He, along with several members of the [[Tech Hunters]], operate a large research operation there.
- When the party arrived to the town adjacent to the observatory, they were attacked by enemies and assisted by [[Richard]].
- The party spent the majority of the session gathering information about the observatory and formulating a plan, as well as gathering useful magic items.
