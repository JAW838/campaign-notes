# Notes
- [[Player Party]] continues to wander through winding floors, hallways, and staircases.
## Evacuated Room
- Come across a room that clearly had equipment removed. After searching the room, [[Richard]] finds a locked box. After smashing it open, 4 kinds of recycled [[Drisalygg]] scroll devices are found within: 4 grenade, 2 flamethrower, 2 shield, 2 fireball.
- In a similar box, a "healing gun" is found.
- These items are recovered and restored items originally found in [[Drisalygg]].
- Some [[Tech Hunters]] research is also recovered.
## Robotics Research
- On the next floor is automaton + robotics research
- An [automaton](https://virtualyoutuber.fandom.com/wiki/Cecilia_Immergreen) with a magical wooden training sword stands in the middle of the room and fights [[Leon Redbrand]], who destroys it.
## Botanical Study Area
- The next floor has lots of strange plants good for potions, poisons, concoctions, etc.
- [[Mike O'Flora]] is elated and begins investigating. He finds a bunch of rare magical plants that [[Sylas Blackstone]] would be very interested in.
- Encountered a shambling mound which was promptly destroyed, although not before engulfing [[Corvel]].
## Crisis
- The group becomes affected by illusions of their greatest fears, caused by the mist filling the hallways. Those affected by the mist vanish.
- [[Corvel]] finds himself running through a foggy alley chasing [[Paraceleus]], unable to retrieve [[Corvel#Threadcleaver|Threadcleaver]], failing both himself and the [[Raven Queen]].
- [[Leon Redbrand]] finds himself running from a creature. He is afraid of himself?
- [[Ondo]] ends up in the twisting halls of a library filled with people making incomprehensible small talk whom he is unable to communicate with. He is afraid of normalcy (losing his whimsy).
- [[Reen Voidswift]] finds himself back at home with his family, with no record of his existence. His unmarked, forgotten grave lies in the backyard. His greatest fear is being forgotten.
- [[Richard]], [[Leone]] and [[Mike O'Flora]] do not suffer illusions. 
- [[Reen Voidswift]] is able to escape his visions on his own.
- [[Corvel]] begins to fight with [[Paraceleus]].
- [[Ondo]] finds a version of himself which is intermingling with the boring people. Then he becomes that version of himself. He sees his math teacher. His vision becomes a collage of all his worst fears. Also there are spiders.
- [[Leone]] scares away the creature creating the mist, causing it to recede to the lower levels of the [[Batel#The Big Tower|Tower]] and revealing the lost teammates.
## Shaft
- Continuing their exploration, they find a rope-suspended elevator that goes up an extremely tall shaft with large columns engraved with symbols whose purpose is channeling ley line energy into the upper parts of the [[Batel#The Big Tower|Tower]].
## Arena
- The elevator stops in a large open space, with gnomish guards pointing weapons at the elevator. [[Theofrastus Bombastus]] is on a balcony with Dee and [[Gestalt]], and reveals the headless enemies controlled by [[Kajal]] were old versions as he unveils the new model.
