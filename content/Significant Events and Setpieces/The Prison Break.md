A major event where the [[Player Party]] infiltrated a prison (location?) holding several prisoners of war to break out [[Sylas]], who was being held there(?).
# The Operation
The prison was located on an island, which the players arrived on by hiding away on a supply ship or using a water breathing potion to hide under it.
To enter the prison proper, some characters, including [[Player Party#Past Members|Stephanie]], hid in objects and smuggled themselves into the prison. Others, including [[Ondo]], infiltrated the prison via a tunnel dug into the mountainside which was later used to funnel . Still others, including [[Diabel]], flew up the side of the prison.
During the operation, many cells were opened to induce confusion and panic, disrupting guard activity.
Near the end of the skirmish, [[Player Party#Past Members|Stephanie]] attempted to reason with a dragon guarding the prison, though this was unsuccessful.
# Results
- Many prisoners were rescued on stolen prison ships, including [[Corvel]], [[Sylas]], and [[Leon Redbrand]]'s brother.
- The drowning of [[Player Party#Past Members|Stephanie]] in her attempted escape.
